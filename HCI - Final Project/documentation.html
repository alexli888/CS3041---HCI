How to Use the Study Timer

Welcome to the Study Timer! This page will guide you on how to use the features of the app.

Timer

The timer helps you track your study sessions. You can set the hours, minutes, and seconds for your session, and the timer will count down. You can start, stop, and reset the timer as needed.

Study Mode

In Study Mode, the app helps you stay focused by managing your breaks and study periods. You can customize how long your study and break intervals are.

Calendar

The Calendar feature allows you to set reminders and plan your study schedule. You can add, edit, and remove events using the drag-and-drop interface.

Menu

The Menu provides access to all the functionalities of the app, allowing easy navigation to the timer, study mode, calendar, and more.
